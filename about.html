What is MMO-Today?

MMO-Today is a news platform designed and created by a guy called Katsumi. It is ment to give you either a quick overview of what is happening in the mmo-world right now, or a more deep look into all the fascinating games and events of which you are a part of.

Who is Katsumi?

Katsumi is the person behinde this website. He designed and created it by the idea of a news platform in the famous manga/anime "Sword Art Online". Of course Katsumi is not his real name, it is, as he is playing mmorpgs, his playertag or playername.

Sword Art Online? Never heard of it.

SPOILER ALERT!
Originaly, Sword Art Online is a manga, but this website refers on the anime. The story is about a boy getting caught in a virtual reality mmorpg (vrmmorpg) along with about 10000 other people. The goal is to finish the game by fighting through 100 levels and beating every boss. The ultimate virtual reality was created in the moment the only gamemaster and creator of the game teleported all players into the first city to anounce, that you wont be able to logout or die in the game, otherwise you will die in reality. Finaly the boy won the game and saved all the people remaining. On this journey he found his big love and together they had to solve many problems even after ending Sword Art Online.
